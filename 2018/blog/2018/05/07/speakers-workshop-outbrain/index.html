Speakers workshop at Outbrain

7th May 2018 / by PyCon Israel Organizing team in General

Speakers workshop at Outbrain took place last Wednesday. Thanks to Gilad de Vries and Outbrain!!

All PyCon Israel CFP submitters got an invitation to attend a workshop about improving presentations skills.

The meeting took place last Wednesday, May 2 at Outbrain offices in Netanya. Gilad de Vries, SVP, Strategy at Outbrain led the session, giving advice on how to make an awesome presentation.

We would like to thank Outbrain for hosting us and Gilad for the great tips.
